1. The Philosophy of Zero Overhead

"If you're still copy-pasting into a chat box, you're working for the AI." - Nat & Matthew Principles

The Masterclass is built on the premise that agents should live where you work: the terminal, the filesystem, and the web. No browser tabs, no manual intervention.
